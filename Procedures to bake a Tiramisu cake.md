PROCEDURES ON HOW TO BAKE AND SERVE A TIRAMISU CAKE

SCOPE
This process applies to individuals preparing a tiramisu cake in a home kitchen environment.

EQUIPMENTS/MATERIALS NEEDED
INGREDIENTS
1. 24–30 ladyfinger biscuits (Savoiardi)
2. 2 cups strong brewed coffee (cooled)
3. 3 large eggs (separated)
4. ½ cup granulated sugar
5. 1 cup mascarpone cheese
6. 1 cup heavy whipping cream
7. 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (unsweetened)
8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
9. 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
